Cookie Policy

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, from remembering your preferences to helping us understand how you use our site.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

Cookie Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Records your cookie preference Persistent
aiway_lang_override Remembers your language preference Persistent

Analytics cookies (optional)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect anonymized data about pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns.

Cookie Purpose Duration
_ga Distinguishes unique users 2 years
_ga_* Maintains session state 2 years

Analytics cookies are only activated when you explicitly accept them through our cookie consent banner.

Managing cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Through our cookie banner — Click “Accept” or “Reject” when prompted, or clear your browser’s local storage to reset your preference.
  • Through your browser — Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-party cookies

Our scheduling system (cal.com) may set its own cookies when you interact with the booking interface. These are governed by Cal.com’s privacy policy.
